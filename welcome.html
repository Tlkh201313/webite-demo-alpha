Northstowe Learning Community

A new chapter for our community

Welcome to Northstowe Secondary College, part of the wider Northstowe Learning Community and Meridian Trust.

This is an exciting time for our students moving schools and we want to ensure that the transfer into the College is both a smooth and stimulating experience for every individual. It is an especially exciting time for us all as we embark upon this journey to open a brand new secondary school and forge our new community. This journey will provide opportunities to build on the strengths and experiences of our community in collaboration with our sister school Swavesey Village College (SVC).

We have been working towards the opening of Northstowe Secondary College since our initial bid to open a new all through 0-19 education campus in 2007–8. It is very exciting for our Trust to now be working with the community in Northstowe and Longstanton, bringing this long standing vision to reality and ensuring that Northstowe Secondary College is an inspiring place to work and learn.
