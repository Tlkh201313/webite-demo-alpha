Latest milestone

Phase 2 launch — Sixth Form & Primary School

Guests gathered at Northstowe Sixth Form to celebrate the official launch of Phase 2 of the Northstowe Learning Community expansion project, with a ribbon-cutting ceremony and guided tour of the brand-new Sixth Form building.

Phase 2 includes a 600-pupil expansion of Northstowe Secondary College, Northstowe Sixth Form (up to 400 places), and Northstowe Primary School with pre-school — developed by Kier for Cambridgeshire County Council and Meridian Trust, with land and funding from Homes England.

26 Mar 2025

Phase 2 official launch

expand_more

Claire Mills, Principal of Northstowe Learning Community: “This is a landmark moment for our school and our community… pupils can now continue their educational journey in a purpose-built environment.”

Mark Dady, Kier Construction: honoured to deliver a high-quality learning environment for staff, students and future generations of Northstowe.

Matthew Brown, Homes England: land and funding supported the existing estate and latest infrastructure, integrated within the town masterplan.

First sixth form and pre-school cohorts started September 2024; next intakes September 2025.

26 Oct 2018

Planning permission granted

expand_more

Cambridgeshire’s largest education campus approved — Phase 1 (~£45m) with secondary school (600 pupils, later expanding to 1,800), SEN school (110 places), sports hall, theatre, and floodlit pitch on the former RAF Oakington site.

Partners: Cambridgeshire County Council, South Cambridgeshire District Council, Homes England, CMAT (now Meridian Trust), Kier Construction and Faithful+Gould. Secondary opening planned September 2019.

Cllr Simon Bywater: “A truly one-of-a-kind, landmark project.” Andy Daly, CMAT: delighted to receive consent and appoint Head of School Carole Moss from January 2019.
