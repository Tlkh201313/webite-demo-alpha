Parental points of contact

Each house has a dedicated office with a phone line and a non-teaching Student Support Assistant available to answer queries. We aim to acknowledge all calls, letters or emails within 24 hours and respond within 48 hours. If you do not hear back in that time, please alert the house office or relevant Assistant Principal.

schedule

Response times: acknowledgement within 24 hours; full response within 48 hours on school days.

Who should I contact?

Use the path below so your enquiry reaches the right person quickly.

1

First contact

For day-to-day or urgent questions about attendance, uniform, rewards and sanctions, contact your house Student Support Assistant. For a specific subject, contact the class teacher. For wellbeing, progress or behaviour, contact the tutor first. See Our Staff for full contact details.

2

Second contact

If concerns about progress in a subject cannot be resolved with the class teacher, contact the subject or curriculum leader. For tutoring, wider progress, wellbeing, safety or potential bullying, contact the senior tutor for your child’s house.

3

Third contact

If your concerns have not been addressed adequately, contact the relevant Assistant Principal or senior leader for your child’s house or the subject area concerned.

House offices

Contact the house office for attendance, day-to-day logistics, and reporting absence when your child is unwell.
